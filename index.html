Welcome to the site! (Posted 8/3/2016)

Special thanks to Mike Weiksner for encouraging me to get a blog up and running - I'll try my best to update on a regular basis, and if I don't, just nag me to do it!

The goal of this site is to share my thoughts and plans regarding the computer science principles course for 7th graders at GCDS, and to inspire a discussion surrounding these topics. I'd like to encourage the discussion to continue both on and offline, and I'll be linking to a Twitter account through which we can share materials, ideas, and opinions.
